About us



We recognise that the world is not always rainbows and sunshine, but we can spread happiness by doing our part. We firmly believe that plants make people happy, thus taking care of one’s mental health is vital! There is no better feeling than getting a thoughtfully chosen plant gift that symbolises you.
Plants and nature play an essential role in our lives, and we recognise how important these plants are to our clients.

We wish to create a love of gardening and growing plants, promote the value of gardens, show how gardening is beneficial for us, and highlight the importance of plants as a small company.
We want to make the UK a greener and more attractive environment by bringing plants into people’s lives. We are dedicated to encouraging everyone to develop.
We work hard at the Krishnachura to bring healthy and gorgeous plants to your lovely home, business, or backyard.

Our greenhouse picks and produces vivid plants, ensuring that each one is healthy, happy, and ready to thrive in your lovely setting.
It is a delight to raise a plant, and we would like to share that joy with everyone, so whether you live in, stop by, and we’ll help you pick a new green friend.

We invite everyone, from amateurs to experienced, to come into our store and choose a plant that speaks to their unique personality. So stop by and say hi! We do not just sell you plants and send you on your way; we like enlightening you on your plant’s origins and how to care for it.

We got all of our expertise by digging our hands into the dirt and asking experts all of the excellent and foolish questions we could think of.
